Sub:- School Education Department - Awarding of promotions to School Assistant & equivalent cadres to the post of Headmasters Gr-II in GVMC / VMC / Municipal Schools / Mpl Corporation Schools on Adhoc basis - Orders – Issued.

<<<>>>

ORDER:

The Government vide reference 4th read above, have issued orders and prescribed norms for re-apportionment of teaching staff under various managements viz., Government, Zilla Parishad/Mandal Parishad and Municipal Schools. As such, instructions have been issued by the Director of School Education, A.P., Amaravati to all Regional Joint Directors of School Education and District Educational Officers in the state to take up promotions from the feeder category of School Assistant & equivalent cadre for the vacant post of Headmaster Gr-II, and feeder category of Secondary Grade Teacher and equivalent cadres for the vacant posts of School Assistant to the extent of need on adhoc basis in accordance with the rules vide reference 5th read above.

Accordingly, certain School Assistants and equivalent cadre working in GVMC / VMC / Municipal Schools / Mpl Corporation Schools have exercised their options through web counselling for promotion to the post of Headmaster Gr-II.

After carefully examining their eligibility, the School Assistants and equivalent cadre as shown in the annexure are hereby promoted to the post of Headmasters Gr-II under Rule 10 (a) of A.P. State and Sub-ordinate Service Rules 1996, read with various orders / instructions issued for promotions on purely adhoc basis.

The above promotions are purely temporary and are liable for reversion at any time without any notice and without assigning any reason thereof for such reversion.

The promotion orders now issued are subject to the following conditions:


  • That their promotion is subject to the outcome of the judgments pending before the Hon’ble High Court/ Supreme Court of India if any.
  • That they shall abide by the terms and conditions stipulated by the Government from time to time and they are liable for any disciplinary action deemed fit for violation thereof if any.

The Mandal Educational Officers/Headmasters concerned are requested to relieve the teachers immediately, for enabling them to join in the promoted place.

The Deputy Educational Officer concerned are requested to allow the individual to join in the promoted place immediately. If the Headmaster (Gr.II) is not joined in the promoted place, cannot claim compulsory wait, under any circumstances for any reason.
